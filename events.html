Storytelling Contest & Prizes

In this event, students had the opportunity to share meaningful experiences from their own lives in front of their peers. The activity encouraged self-expression, confidence, and active listening, as each student reflected on a personal story and its significance. To celebrate participation and creativity, every storyteller received a bookmark as a prize, making the event both inspiring and memorable for everyone involved.

Club Rush Participation

During Club Rush, the Language & Literature Club introduced both students and their parents to our activities and goals. We set up an engaging booth where parents could learn about the club, ask questions, and even participate in a fun game designed to showcase our focus on language and literature. The interactive approach made the experience memorable, helped parents understand what we do, and encouraged new students to join the club.
