L.C.C.S

A space for students to grow in reading, writing, and speaking.

The Language & Literature Club is a student-run organization at L.C.C.S where members practice vocabulary, improve essay writing, explore books, and share creative work. Whether you’re preparing for exams, building your writing skills, or just love literature — this site is your place.Open to all grades and skill levels — you don’t need to be an expert reader or writer.

Purpose/Goals

  • Improve Language Skills: Practice vocabulary, grammar, reading comprehension, and essay writing.
  • Encourage Creativity: Members can write poems, short stories, essays, or even reviews.
  • Promote Reading & Discussion: Weekly book discussions, themed reading challenges, or group debates.
  • Share Work & Feedback: Submit essays or stories online, and get constructive feedback from peers.
